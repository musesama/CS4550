Very Dumb Blog Website Design

User

- As a user, Adam first visits the home page then click "No account yet?" link. This brings him to the new account form, where he enters his email address, username and password. After submitting the form, his registered email address will get the confirmation email. Upon clicking the link in the mail, his new account is ready to use.


- After Adam has registered the account, he visits the home page again and enter the username and password in the given input areas, then he clicks the button "Log in", where he will be directed to the homepage with a logged in status showing on where it was the login form.


- After Adam has logged in, he is able to post new "twitter" message with no character limits in the posting new feeds form. This should be considered as an article if exceeds to over 200 chars.


- As a logged in user, Adam can comments under any "twitter" message or "blog" by input his comments in the reply block, then hit "Comment" to submit. Then he will be able to see his own comment. He can also reply to others' comments by clicking the "reply" following each comments.


- As a logged in user, Adam can follow other user by clicking "follow" after he moved his mouse over the user's nickname(to be set in "setting"). He can also like any blogs or messages by clicking the heart that follows the message or blog.


Resources

Blogs or Tweets

- Poster's nickname: The appeared nickname of the poster
- Posting date/time: The exact date and time this message was posted.
- Comments and Likes: The numbers of comments and likes for this message.

Personal Center

- Account Settings: Change password.(More options could be added)
- Profile Settings: Change profile image, nickname, date of birth, other profile info.
- My Blogs/Tweets: A list of messages what the user has posted.
- My Comments/Likes: A list of comments what the user has posted. Likes are under different tab.
